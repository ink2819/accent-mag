 *A digital publication for contemporary literature, art, and interdisciplinary cultural practice.*

**Accent Accent Magazine** is a digital publication devoted to contemporary literature,
visual art, performance, design, and interdisciplinary cultural practice, with a special
focus on bilingual and diasporic voices across China, Asia, and the global creative scene.

The magazine publishes book reviews, in-depth interviews with writers and artists,
original artworks, critical essays, and selected scripts from the Accent Podcast. It
operates in a continuous, open-ended publishing format.

## Chinese Disrupters
*Redefining the Global Creative Edge*

One of the magazine’s planned sections, **Chinese Disrupters**, will spotlight a new
generation of artists, writers, publishers, and cultural innovators redefining what
“Chinese” means in a global context.
